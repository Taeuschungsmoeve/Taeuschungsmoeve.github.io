Bagworm Moth Caterpillar

The bagworm moth caterpillar uses variations of short and long sticks to assemble their dwellings in a meticulous pattern. The larvae collect and assemble sticks of varying length and assemble them in a unique pattern, creating a house that spirals to a tip.
